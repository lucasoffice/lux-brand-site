Scope

Selective demo, new walls, electrical relocation, finishes — furniture excluded.

Eight included line items, five excluded. Floor 1 runs ~70% epoxy-painted concrete and ~30% carpeted offices. Floor 2 runs specialty white VCT rubber at watchmaker stations with carpet tile elsewhere.

Included — Eight Items
  1. Selective demolition~1 week per floor, standard hours.
  2. New wall construction250 LF per floor budgeted, pending plan finalization.
  3. Electrical relocationLump-sum per floor. No new service, no major upgrades.
  4. Floor 1 flooring~70% epoxy over concrete, ~30% carpet tile in offices.
  5. Floor 2 flooringWhite VCT rubber at watchmaker stations, carpet tile elsewhere.
  6. PaintCoat throughout renovated areas. No wallpaper.
  7. ACT patchingSalvage and reuse existing grid. Patch around new walls.
  8. Phasing & logisticsFive dumpsters per phase. Freight elevator coordination included.
Excluded — Five Items
  1. Furniture & workstationsStainless workbenches and jeweler stations under separate vendor scope.
  2. Architectural drawings$15–$20K allowance, client responsibility if permit triggered.
  3. Floor 3Descoped per Chris. Retained in rollup for reference only.
  4. WallpaperPaint only per client direction.
  5. PermittingAssumed not required. Sprinkler and electrical thresholds pending electrician review.
