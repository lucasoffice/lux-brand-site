02
Scope

Demo, walls, electrical, flooring, paint, ACT — Floors 1 and 2 only.

Ten line items in. Five out. Specialty white rubberized VCT in watchmaker zones, sealed concrete across 70% of Floor 1 storage.

Included — 10 Items
  1. Interior demolition~1 week per floor, off-hours, dust containment to active zones.
  2. Wall reconfiguration250 LF per floor, partition spec pending schematic lock.
  3. Electrical relocationLump sum, $130K/floor.
  4. Carpet tile flooring20"×20" tile, $9/SF including adhesive.
  5. White rubberized VCT Spec OwedWatchmaker zones, Floor 2. Dropped components must bounce, not break.
  6. Epoxy concrete~70% of Floor 1, sealed and painted for storage flow.
  7. Paint, full coatThroughout renovated areas, included in $56/SF labor.
  8. ACT patch and replaceSalvage existing where viable, replace where damaged.
  9. Dumpsters and debris~5 per phase, demo and finish-out cycles.
  10. Phasing and protectionSix phases, half-floor at a time, overlapped.
Excluded — 5 Items
  1. Furniture and workstationsStainless benches and jeweler stations, separate vendor.
  2. Stainless steel workbenchesFabricated off-site, client-sourced.
  3. Architectural drawings$15K–$20K allowance if permit triggers filing.
  4. Permit feesBase assumes finishes-only classification, no permit.
  5. Sprinkler modificationsReassessed if wall layout triggers relocation.

Approach

Off-hours demo, daytime build, half-floor phasing, direct GC contract.

Six-phase delivery with one-week overlaps. Demo runs after-hours. Lux contracts directly with TAG Heuer — no PM intermediary.
